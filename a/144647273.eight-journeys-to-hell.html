Wholeness

I am scattered, a kaleidoscope shattered Prismatic self spun into myriad shards
Slicing at my being from all angles As I desperately clutch at illusions of unity

A shrill inner committee bellows Their ceaseless decrees and accusations "Too ugly!" "Too soft!" "Unforgivable!" Disowning any truth that doesn't obey

Behind the barricades, cages, and locked rooms Other selves gut-punched into submission cower Mouthing their howls through socketed silence I avert my gaze, though the wails persist

At my core, a wasteland, scorched, vacant Where a brilliant diamond of coherence once throned Now a dull relic, cracked, its fragments Strewn as forsaken stars through these desolate lands

My every gesture dredged from conflict's well Opposing selves warring, feinting, defrauding An exhausting militia with no peace in sight Until I behold this shrapnel as my face

To taste the elixir of unity again I must become the intrepid archeologist
Dusting off and exalting each abandoned relic Re-embracing them as this one ragged precious mind

Authenticity

Down, down the labyrinth's gullet I'm swallowed

Into cavernous masquerades, fashioned to guise

My every breath, a masterwork of cloaks

Tailored to each stage, concealing the genesis

In the Canyon of Projected Ideals

I adorn towering false effigies of my worth

Behind panoplies of bravado and hollow claims

While the true pearl of being asphyxiates

Slipping through the Arcade of Personas

A frantic juggler and performer of selves

Every window I veil, each mask finely starched

Lest the raw light of authenticity breach

Into the Gothic Theater of Doubts we spiral

Where brutal ushers flog any blossoming shoots

Of uninvented honest human experience

With flayings of "Who are you to...?"

Until at last the Path of Un-being gapes

A haunting byway strewn with hollow rinds

The discarded costumes of each failed safe-self

That too proved an unsustainable fiction

Here at the trail's mortuary end, a cave mouth

Wherefrom strange unvarnished whisperings drift

Beckoning me to emerge naked, maskless

Into the terrifying reveal of what simply is

Presence

A runaway train, hurtling

From future thoughts to pasts re-lived

Rattling whistle-stop recollections, ruminations

Never here, never here

The pounding drums of anxiety

Score this manic brakeless trajectory

As each moment's time-stamped essence

Blurs unsampled, ungrappled

Wheels clacking out garbled Morse panic

Over how, when, where and why

But never chiming the still point's secret

Of bare being, resting aware

Bypassing vistas kaleidoscopic

Dramas, revelations in each lowly glimpse

While this rickety engine races on

Disconnecting, avoiding, check please

This fevered careening needs stillness's station

A depot to un-board, inhabit the breath

Feel these wheels merge with the rails

And arrive in belonging's polestar

Fluidity

Taxidermied emotions on morbid display

In vitrines of propriety, propriety

No lusty breathings defog these cases

Just still-life facsimiles, mere pale traceries

No oceanic swellings may crest Here

No murky undertows disturb the surface

Each churning fizz stray of the accepted
Sedated, inerted, lest Chaos interlope

Behind crystalline caging, atrophied

The wild array of our rainbow-streamed truth

Anger's searing auric clouds shuttered away

While ecstasies petrify to tepid vignettes

Tragedy's thunderhead, shunted off-stage

Lest its rumbling triggers upend our frail sets
Of decorous veneers, masking the truth

That our hearts pulsate sacred deluges

From this museum gallery we gaze out
At a papier-mâché world of stifled normalcy
But the windows only stale light outwards
No Entry for the elemental forces to flow

We curate and preserve this dreary diorama

Lest the dam rupture, drowning our brittle safe-room

In rude reminders that we are undying radiances

Whose foaming truths haunt every false crystalline tomb

Compassion

You mewling refuse, squirming in frailties

Do you mistake this peripatetic cesspit for a haven?
There are no excuses, only consequences
For the innumerable ways you disturb and disappoint

With each failure to remit full dividends
From your luckless fortunes, you profane

And each compromise of your pristine potential
Billows as an unbearable stain that cries:"Unclean!"

All your pitiable desires for grace or compassion

Are infantile tics, desperate playgrounds

For peddling the hallucinatory disease called "humanity"

To which I maintain a ruthless, miraculous immunity

An isthmus of disgust separates our realms

Where I decree always from the unerring plane
Of flawless ideals, unmarred by empathy's grotesqueries

Judging with searing equanimity each deformity you name

So cull the endless mitigation committees

Vying to excuse your litanies of unforgivable

I require no pretexts to raze you utterly
My fire delving from a purity your cowering can't unsettled

Equanimity

Howling for the hallucined rapture I convulse, mouth-ing anguished pleas To each fleeting chrism promising respite
From these serrating oceans of craving's thirst

A momentary relief blooms in the offing But the urge-winds capsize its mirage
Violently pitch-poleing me back under Into the churning needs, dreads, have-to-haves

In panicked grasping I flail for buoyancy Any flotsam -- achievements, purchases, prized-names
Clawing to browbeat back the ravenous hungry truth: No harbor permits lasting, no state is safe-

For the gravity-well of discontentment
Fatally warps each temporary driftwood
Subsuming shapes of respite into itself Its graviton-teeth gnawjng all repose

Even the bliss-masts of transcendent epiphanies Wrenched horizontal by the implacable gravities That bay to concede all moorings hopeless
And surrender into free-fall's obliterating

To crest even briefly, equanimity's blue peninsula
Requires amputation of these raptor-urgencies Perpetually vulturing for sacrificial assuagements To throw into their bottomless consumptive maws

Dynamism

A crypt-hush weight mercurial as grave-silt Muffles the halls where gelid airs cess-pool
Intensity's throbs stilled to steady flatlines
In the vaults where unlived passions hie

Desiccated husks of buddings never quickened
Dreams shriveled on referential vines hang
Alongside dray-weights of inert intuitions
Drained amniotic impulses Grayed before whelmed

In the corners, pale spoor of untapped strengths
Resolute fonts leeched of all fervor and sap
Guttered semillances, latencies aborted
At the first brutal pangs of severance deformed

Upon walls pocked with fallow pock-mouths Wintered seeds of unrisen verities scar
Stasis coronates this denied abattoir
Where still-bourne prospects decay untested

Here pristine uglios shed from asphyxiated wholes Mat in snuffer-folds replete with neutered power
Psyche amortized to pure subjunctive
Relic'd in this cloistral boneyard of unlived fire

Transcendence

Within these oubliette walls No wick of numinous mystery Filters through the slits Just the dank tincture of self, self, self

A cramped ossuary of mirrored prides Deflecting any rays that might dissolve
These fortressed delusions of singular reign
Over territories microcosmically puny

Every Adjacent ponders my cult's reflection Whether crudely adversary or sycophant Their deference, their rejection both shore up
The tiny nexus I franchise as "The Real"

Beyond these parapets, rumored Outer Realms
Myth-spun vacancies absent my famed rulership I squint to discern, but the radiant truths there Scald with hints of my own cosmic insignificance

So I retreat, revering my shadowed citadel Never idolatrying those voids of impersonal light For here in the dank chapels of iamfulness I mawkishly preside as Nav'lic Pope and Czar

Deaf to offers of exodus to the All-in-All
I double-down in this sepulchral holdfast
Better enjoy my contracted forever
Than hazard dissolution into That which outstrips

Addendum: A healthy psyche’s attributes

A fully integrated and healthy psyche that has metabolized all experiences and aspects of itself carries some powerful characteristics:

Wholeness - A deep sense of being an undivided, cohesive whole human being without disowned parts. All qualities and experiences have been consciously embraced.

Authenticity - The ability to be profoundly authentic and live from one's innate truth without need for pretense, persona or hiding aspects of oneself.

Presence - An embodied, grounded presence and availability to meet reality and experience exactly as it is without resistance or dissociation.

Fluidity - A flexibility to fluidly experience and express the full range of emotions, states and archetypal energies as appropriate without fragmentation.

Compassion - Having re-owned all judgments towards self and others, resulting in an empathic understanding of the shared human condition.

Equanimity - An abiding inner equanimity, acceptance and freedom from being psychologically buffeted by fears, desires or external circumstances.

Dynamism - A vibrant creative dynamism and zest for exploration fueled by the constant interplay of integrated psychic opposites.

Transcendence - A sense of at-onement with one's deeper nature beyond just personality, facilitating access to expansive transpersonal awareness.

Introduction

In our daily lives, we often perceive reality as a collection of distinct points: a tree, a thought, a feeling, a star. We categorize and compartmentalize, creating neat boxes for the chaotic wonder that surrounds us. But what if this pointillist view is obscuring a deeper, more breathtaking truth? What if, instead of points, reality is inherently spectral? Imagine the act of spectrification, not as division, but as revelation - an unveiling of continuous gradations where we once saw only separation.

Spectrify to gain clarity

To “spectrify” is to introduce a spectral perspective, to analyze something not as isolated points, but as a continuum of variation. Consider leadership, often presented in binary terms: autocratic or democratic. Yet, through spectrification, we perceive a rich spectrum stretching between these poles, an endless array of nuanced styles, each blending into the next. This spectral lens, however, isn't limited to leadership styles; it can be applied to the very fabric of existence, especially when we consider the profound concept of Glory.

Glory is revealed in diverse form

Glory, acts as an “ambiguator”, that conceptual umbrella unifying seemingly disparate forms of excellence. It’s the "height of positive characteristic," manifesting in diverse forms like Brilliance, Gentleness, and Majesty. We conventionally perceive these as distinct glories - the glory of a sharp mind, the glory of a kind heart, the glory of a towering mountain. But spectrification urges us to see further. What if reality itself is a spectrified glory? Not just scattered instances, but a continuous, flowing spectrum of excellence. From the faintest glimmer of kindness to the most overwhelming display of majesty, it's all interconnected, gradations within a single, awe-inspiring field.

Imagine spectrifying "Brilliance." It’s not just a point on a graph, but a spectrum stretching from subtle intelligence to incandescent genius, each point on that spectrum radiating a unique flavor of brilliance. Now, expand this spectrification to all forms of Glory - Gentleness, Majesty, and beyond. Suddenly, reality transforms from a collection of separate glories into a magnificent, spectrified symphony of Glory. Every phenomenon, every being, every moment exists somewhere along this vast, continuous spectrum of excellence, each contributing to the grand, unified composition.

Cultivation of awe through spectrification

And what is the natural human response to witnessing such a spectacle? It is Awe. Awe, as defined, is that profound emotion of reverence, wonder, experienced in response to witnessing Glory in its various forms. When we adopt a spectral perspective, when we actively spectrify our perception of reality, we open ourselves to a deeper, more pervasive Awe. No longer are we limited to isolated moments of wonder; instead, we perceive the continuous, spectral Glory inherent in everything. We begin to understand that the seemingly mundane, the seemingly ordinary, are not devoid of Glory, but simply residing at a different point on the spectrum, still contributing to the breathtaking whole.

By embracing spectrification, we move beyond a fragmented, point-based understanding of the world. We step into a reality revealed as a continuous, interconnected, and profoundly glorious spectrum. And in this spectrified symphony of Glory, we find not only deeper understanding, but also a boundless wellspring of Awe, forever enriching our perception of existence itself.
